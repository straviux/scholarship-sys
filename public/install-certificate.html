📋 Manual Installation Instructions (Alternative)

  1. Click "Download Certificate" or "Download Auto-Installer"
  2. For Auto-Installer: Double-click the downloaded .ps1 or .bat file
  3. For Manual: Double-click rootCA.pem and follow steps below
  4. Click "Install Certificate..."
  5. Select "Current User" and click "Next"
  6. Choose "Place all certificates in the following store"
  7. Click "Browse" and select "Trusted Root Certification Authorities"
  8. Click "Next" then "Finish"
  9. Click "Yes" on the security warning
  10. Restart your browser completely
  11. Click "Verify Installation" to check if the certificate was installed correctly
